Prepositions of time

Complete the sentences. Use words from the list:

ago, during, for, from, in, since, to

1. The cell phone was invented over 30 years ________.

a. ago
b. since
c. during

2. The first laptop was produced ________ 1981.

a. to
b. in
c. for

3. Mexico has been independent ________ more than 200 years.

a. since
b. from
c. for

4. World war II lasted ________ 1939 ________ 1945.

a. from / to
b. in / to
c. during / in

5. Vietnam was separated into two parts ________ about 30 years.

a. since
b. during
c. for
